# KaltrixOS — Africa's Business Operating System

## What Is KaltrixOS?
KaltrixOS is a business credibility and discovery platform built 
for African SMEs. Businesses register, get verified, and become 
discoverable to customers — while KaltrixOS identifies digital 
gaps and connects them to Kaltrix agency services.

## The Problem
40M+ SMEs in Nigeria have no functional online presence.
Customers cannot find, verify, or transact with them online.
Businesses lose revenue daily to more digitally visible competitors.

## The Solution
KaltrixOS gives every business:
- A verified, searchable business profile
- A TrustScore that builds customer confidence
- Analytics showing who is searching for them
- A direct path to full digital services via Kaltrix Agency

## How It Makes Money

2. Growth plan — ₦25,000/month
3. Pro plan — ₦60,000/month
4. Agency services — custom pricing

## Tech Stack
- Frontend/Backend: Next.js 14 (App Router)
- Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL)
- Auth: Supabase Auth
- Payments: Paystack
- Email: Resend
- Hosting: Vercel

## The Kaltrix Agency Layer
Every business on KaltrixOS with a low TrustScore or missing
website is a flagged lead inside the Kaltrix admin dashboard.
The agency reaches out with a tailored offer. The platform
feeds the agency. The agency elevates the platform.
